How it works

We provide a sustainable solution helping SMEs (sellers) to finance their invoices comfirmed by the issuing vendors ahead of time and an alternative way for investors to earn and diversify their income.

Sellers

are verified SMEs who require quick funds to do business and offer to sell their payment invoice confirmed by the issuing vendor to potential buyers on the platform at a certain discount.

Investors

are individuals, organizations or lending firms who have spare funds to lend Sellers with verified invoice within 120 days of payment and get rewarded with certain percentage of interest.

Vendors

are organizations that are directly involved in a business transaction with sellers whose invoices are being sold. Vendors have to confirm invoices before they can be sold on Quidroo.

Invoice Upload

After completing registration and confirming email, Seller uploads an invoice onto the platform

Payment to Seller

Once bidding ends and Investor is picked, their wallet is debited and the funds credited to the Seller’s wallet

Invoice Verification

Vendor acknowledges the particular Seller, verifies, approves and consents to the invoicing transaction on the platform

Payment to Quidroo

The Vendor pays Quidroo the invoice amount on the due date

Invoice Bidding

Investors bid for an invoice, and investor with the least interest rate is picked

Payment to Investor

Quidroo credits the wallet of the Investor including the due interest

About Us

Quidroo is an online platform that enables SMEs get paid faster by selling their invoices to get fast cash offers at favorable rates. It is a sustainable solution helping SMEs to finance their invoices ahead of time and an alternative way for investors to earn and diversify their income.

We help SMEs finance their A/R and invoices to unlock their cash flows and fund their growth and short term cash flow problems.

Connecting invoice and A/R sellers to a network of buyers and investors, our platform offers a fast, cost-effective, secure and automated environment that uses a patented in-house, incentive-driven credit rating system that includes both public and privately derived company data.
